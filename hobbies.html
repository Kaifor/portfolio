Hobbies
Building PCs
I have been building PCs since I was 12, I've lost count on how many I've built. I have a love and passion for physical hardware and how it works. Learning how HDDs magnetise the smallest portions to either negative or positive and map them out to be read as 0 or 1s, how our CPU is fed electricity and how far computers have come since the first logic gates were built to demonstrate what we now know as a AND/XOR/OR functions. I will always enjoy learning about and spending some of my free time with electronics of all kinds.
